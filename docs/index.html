Our Reminders

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Our Reminders is a couples reminder app built by Ken Fook Choong. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Your privacy matters. The app is designed to be simple and transparent — there are no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

1. What Data We Collect

The app collects only the minimum information needed to function:

  • Display name — a name you choose when you join (not your real name unless you want it to be).
  • Reminders — the content of reminders you and your partner create.
  • Push notification token — a device identifier used solely to deliver push notifications.
  • Couple code — a randomly generated code used to link you with your partner.

The app does not collect your email address, password, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the core features of the app:

  • Storing and displaying reminders between you and your partner.
  • Sending push notifications when reminders are created or due.
  • Linking your account with your partner through a shared couple code.

Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or profiling purposes.

3. Push Notifications

The app uses push notifications to alert you about reminders. When you grant notification permission, a device token is stored so that notifications can be delivered to your device.

You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings. If you do, the app will continue to work but you will not receive alerts.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access is restricted through row-level security policies so that only you and your partner can see your shared reminders.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by leaving the couple within the app. When you leave, your profile and associated data are removed.

If you would like to request complete deletion of all your data, please contact us at the email address listed below.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Reminders is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
